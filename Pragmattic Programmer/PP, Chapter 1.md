### Points to Remember Everyday
1. learn a at least a 1 new language every year.
2. Read one technical book every month, Ex. *The kind of technologies you are working on*
3. Read one non-technical book too.
4. Take classes (?)
5. Stay real to the latest news about the field that you are in. *For us it's programming for now*

### How to think Critically
1. Ask *why* five times.
2. Ask about the context of the problem.
3. Understand the circumstances of the solution. *When and where would this work*
